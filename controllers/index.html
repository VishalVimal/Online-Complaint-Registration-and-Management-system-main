About FixIt

FixIt is a cutting-edge digital platform designed to bridge the gap between citizens and local authorities. We believe that every community issue deserves attention and resolution. Our mission is to make civic engagement seamless, transparent, and effective. With FixIt, reporting infrastructure problems, tracking complaint status, and seeing real change in your community has never been easier. We've built an intuitive system that empowers residents to take action, while providing authorities with the tools they need to respond efficiently and maintain accountability.

How It Works

Report Community Issues Instantly

From broken streetlights to potholes, water leaks to waste management problems - report any community issue with just a few clicks. Upload photos, add location details, and provide descriptions to help authorities understand and prioritize your concerns. Our streamlined process ensures your complaint reaches the right department quickly.

Track Progress in Real-Time

Stay informed every step of the way. Monitor your complaint's journey from submission to resolution. Receive updates when your complaint is reviewed, when work begins, and when it's completed. Transparency is at the heart of what we do - you'll always know the status of your reported issues and can see the impact of your civic engagement.
